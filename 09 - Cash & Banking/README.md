# SOP 09 – Cash & Banking

## Overview
This folder contains the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and
supporting tools governing bank reconciliations, cash monitoring, and
bank account controls.

The objective is to ensure completeness, accuracy, safeguarding of
cash, and timely identification of discrepancies.

## Contents

### SOP
• **SOP_09_Cash_and_Banking.docx**  
Defines responsibilities, reconciliation procedures, and review
requirements.

### Operational Checklist
• **SOP_09_Cash_and_Banking_Checklist.xlsx**  
A structured checklist used to prepare, validate, and review bank
reconciliations and cash controls.

## Key Areas Covered
• Bank statement sourcing  
• GL vs bank reconciliation  
• Outstanding item investigation  
• Subsequent clearance  
• Unusual transaction monitoring  
• Authority over bank signatories  

## How This Is Used
The SOP defines governance expectations, while the checklist is
completed each reporting cycle to evidence reconciliation and review.

## Outputs
• Approved bank reconciliations  
• Documented reconciling items  
• Confirmed cash balances  

## Standards
IFRS – Accrual Basis of Accounting  
ISA – Audit Evidence Principles
