## Control Principles Embedded in This SOP

### 1) Protect the Asset
Cash requires the strongest control environment.

### 2) Segregation of Duties
Preparation and approval must be separate.

### 3) Timely Reconciliation
Differences must be identified and cleared quickly.

### 4) Access Governance
Bank rights must be controlled and reviewed.

### 5) Visibility
Management must understand liquidity at all times.

---

## Core Activities in This SOP

### Bank Governance
- maintain approved bank account list  
- ensure mandates are current  
- periodically review user access  

### Cash Receipts
- record promptly  
- allocate to customers  
- resolve unidentified items  

### Payments
- follow approval limits  
- segregate preparation and authorisation  
- retain proof of payment  

### Bank Reconciliations
- prepare monthly or more frequently  
- investigate reconciling items  
- obtain reviewer sign-off  

### Cashflow Monitoring
- track daily balances  
- prepare short-term forecasts  
- escalate risks  

### Fraud Controls
- monitor unusual transactions  
- maintain strict payment authorisation  
- control banking access
