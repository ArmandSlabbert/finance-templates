# SOP 06 – Payroll & Independent Contractors

## Overview
Standard Operating Procedure governing payroll processing and
independent contractor payments, including statutory deductions,
submissions, and reconciliations.

## Key Areas
• Payroll calculations and review
• PAYE, UIF, SDL compliance
• Contractor classification controls
• Payment approvals and reconciliations
• Audit and record retention

## Standards
BCEA, LRA, Income Tax Act, UIF Act, SARS Payroll Guides
