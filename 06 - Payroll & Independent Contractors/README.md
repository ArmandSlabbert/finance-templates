# SOP 06 – Payroll & Independent Contractors

## Overview
This folder contains the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and
supporting tools governing payroll processing and payments to
independent contractors.

The objective is to ensure accuracy of calculations, compliance with
statutory requirements, appropriate approvals, and a complete audit
trail prior to payment.

## Contents

### SOP
• **SOP_06_Payroll_and_Independent_Contractors.docx**  
Defines payroll governance, responsibilities, statutory obligations,
and approval requirements.

### Operational Checklist
• **Payroll_and_Independent_Contractors_Checklist.xlsx**  
A structured control checklist used to validate payroll inputs,
calculations, approvals, and payment execution.

## Key Areas Covered
• Employee master file management  
• Timesheet and contract validation  
• PAYE, UIF, and SDL calculations  
• Net pay verification  
• Contractor invoice validation  
• Delegation-of-authority approval  
• GL posting accuracy  

## How This Is Used
The SOP provides the control framework, while the checklist is used
operationally each pay cycle to ensure payroll accuracy and compliance.

Completed checklists are retained as statutory and audit evidence.

## Outputs
• Approved payroll  
• Confirmed payments  
• Reconciled payroll control accounts  

## Standards
Income Tax Act (South Africa)  
Basic Conditions of Employment Act
