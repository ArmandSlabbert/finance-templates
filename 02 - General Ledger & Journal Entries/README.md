# SOP 02 – General Ledger & Journal Entries

## Overview
This folder contains the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and
supporting tools governing the preparation, review, approval, and
posting of general ledger journal entries.

The objective is to ensure journals are accurate, valid, appropriately
authorised, and fully supported, maintaining the integrity of the
general ledger and financial statements.

## Contents

### SOP
• **SOP_02_General_Ledger_and_Journal_Entries.docx**  
Defines the policies, controls, and responsibilities for journal
identification, preparation, review, approval, and posting.

### Operational Checklist
• **General_Ledger_and_Journal_Entries_Checklists.xlsx**  
A structured checklist and journal register used to control journal
processing, track review and approval, and evidence completion for each
reporting period.

## Key Areas Covered
• Journal identification and preparation  
• Reporting period cut-off controls  
• GL account and cost centre accuracy  
• Balanced journal entries (debits = credits)  
• Reasonableness and validation checks  
• Supporting documentation and audit trail  
• Review, approval, and segregation of duties  
• Post-posting validation and issue escalation  

## How This Is Used
The SOP provides the control framework for journal processing, while the
checklist is used operationally during each reporting cycle to track
completion, review, and approval.

Completed checklists and registers are retained as part of the
month-end close documentation and audit support files.

## Outputs
• Accurate and controlled general ledger  
• Approved journal entries with supporting evidence  
• Journal register for the reporting period  

## Standards
IFRS – Accrual Basis of Accounting  
IAS 1 – Presentation of Financial Statements
