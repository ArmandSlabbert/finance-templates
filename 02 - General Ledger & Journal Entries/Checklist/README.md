# Balance Sheet Reconciliations

## Overview
This repository contains the framework, procedures, and operational tools used to prepare, review, and evidence **balance sheet reconciliations** within a controlled month-end environment.

The objective is to ensure that all balances reported in the general ledger are:

- valid  
- complete  
- accurately valued  
- properly supported  
- independently reviewed  

The methodology supports strong financial governance, audit readiness, and reliable reporting.

## Scope of Reconciliations

Typical areas include:

- Bank and cash  
- Accounts receivable and payable  
- VAT / payroll liabilities  
- Accruals and provisions  
- Intercompany balances  
- Loans and equity  
- Prepayments and other assets

## Evidence Standards

Reconciliations should typically include:

- GL extracts  
- supporting schedules  
- third-party confirmations (where relevant)  
- explanations for reconciling items  
- ageing of outstanding differences
