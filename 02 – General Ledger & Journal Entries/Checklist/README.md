# General Ledger & Journal Entries – Checklist

## Overview
This file provides a structured checklist and register to control and
evidence the preparation, review, approval, and posting of general
ledger journal entries.

The checklist is designed to reduce the risk of errors, misstatements,
and unauthorised postings, while supporting strong governance and
audit readiness.

## File Contents
• **General_Ledger_and_Journal_Entries_Checklists.xlsx**

The workbook includes:
- A detailed journal preparation and validation checklist
- A journal register to track all journals posted during a reporting
  period
- Built-in controls for review, approval, and status tracking

## Key Control Areas Covered
• Correct reporting period (cut-off)  
• Clear journal descriptions and business rationale  
• Accurate GL account and cost centre allocation  
• Balanced journal entries (debits = credits)  
• Reasonableness checks against prior period, budget, or expectations  
• Supporting documentation and audit trail  
• Review, approval, and segregation of duties  

## How This Fits Into the Finance Operating Manual
• **SOP 02 – General Ledger & Journal Entries** defines the policy and
  internal control framework  
• This checklist provides practical execution evidence and reviewer
  sign-off  

Together, they support consistent, controlled, and auditable journal
processing.

## Usage
The checklist is completed during each reporting cycle and retained as
part of the month-end close documentation and audit support files.

The journal register is maintained to provide a complete record of all
journals processed for the period.

## Intended Audience
• Finance Managers  
• Financial Accountants  
• Financial Controllers  
• Internal and External Auditors
