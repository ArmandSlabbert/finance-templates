## Overview
This directory contains the official Standard Operating Procedure governing the preparation, support, review, and approval of balance sheet reconciliations.

The objective is to ensure balances reported in the general ledger are:

- accurate  
- complete  
- valid  
- fully supported  

This is one of the most important financial control activities in the close process.

---

## What This SOP Covers
The procedure establishes mandatory standards for:

- Reconciliation format and requirements  
- Agreement to the general ledger  
- Treatment of reconciling items  
- Review and sign-off  
- Required supporting evidence  
- Escalation of exceptions  

It applies to **all balance sheet accounts** across **all reporting periods**.

## Control Principles Embedded in This SOP

### 1) GL Agreement
Every reconciliation must tie to the ledger.

### 2) Evidence-Based Support
Balances must be provable from source documentation.

### 3) Independent Review
Preparation and approval must be segregated.

### 4) Timely Resolution
Reconciling items are tracked, aged, and cleared.

### 5) Escalation
Material or unusual items require management attention.
