# Finance Operating Manual

## Overview
This Finance Operating Manual defines the policies, procedures, and
internal controls governing the finance function. It provides a
structured framework to ensure accurate financial reporting, strong
governance, regulatory compliance, and effective decision support.

The manual is designed to support operational finance, audit readiness,
and management oversight across the full financial reporting cycle.

## Objectives
• Ensure consistency and accuracy in financial processes  
• Support compliance with accounting standards and legislation  
• Strengthen internal controls and governance  
• Provide clear ownership, review, and escalation pathways  
• Enable audit-ready financial reporting  

## Scope
This manual applies to all finance processes including:
• Period-end close and reporting  
• General ledger management  
• Revenue recognition  
• Tax and statutory compliance  
• Payroll and contractor payments  
• Cash and banking  
• Balance sheet controls  
• Audit evidence and document retention

## Roles & Accountability
Each SOP clearly defines:
• Process ownership  
• Execution responsibilities  
• Review and approval requirements  
• Escalation thresholds  

Overall accountability for the Finance Operating Manual rests with the
Finance Manager.

## Standards & Frameworks
This manual aligns with:
• IFRS and accrual accounting principles  
• IAS 1 – Presentation of Financial Statements  
• COSO Internal Control Framework  
• King IV Report on Corporate Governance  
• Applicable tax and labour legislation  

## Audit & Compliance
The procedures defined in this manual support:
• Internal and external audit requirements  
• Statutory reporting and compliance  
• Consistent audit evidence and documentation  
• Transparent issue escalation and remediation  

## Review & Maintenance
The Finance Operating Manual is reviewed annually or when:
• Accounting standards change  
• Legislation is updated  
• Business operations or risk profiles change  

Updates must be approved by Finance Management.
