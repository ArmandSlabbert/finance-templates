# SOP 11 – Controls & Escalation

## Overview
This folder contains the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and
supporting tools governing identification, escalation, and resolution
of financial control issues.

The objective is to ensure risks are promptly communicated, ownership
is assigned, and corrective actions are tracked to closure.

## Contents

### SOP
• **SOP_11_Controls_and_Escalation.docx**  
Defines escalation thresholds, responsibilities, communication
protocols, and documentation standards.

### Operational Checklist
• **SOP_11_Controls_and_Escalation_Checklist.xlsx**  
A structured issue log used to track financial control failures,
management awareness, and resolution progress.

## Key Areas Covered
• Financial reporting risks  
• Tax compliance breaches  
• Cash anomalies  
• Payroll issues  
• Documentation gaps  
• Fraud indicators  

## How This Is Used
Issues are recorded when identified and tracked through investigation,
resolution, and closure, ensuring transparency and accountability.

## Outputs
• Risk visibility  
• Documented corrective actions  
• Closure confirmation
