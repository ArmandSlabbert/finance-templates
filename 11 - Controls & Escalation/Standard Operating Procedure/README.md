## Overview
This directory contains the official Standard Operating Procedure governing the internal control framework and escalation protocols across finance.

The objective is to ensure issues are:

- identified early  
- assessed correctly  
- escalated appropriately  
- resolved in a timely manner  
- documented for governance and audit  

Strong escalation processes are fundamental to accountability.

---

## What This SOP Covers
The procedure establishes mandatory rules for:

- Control ownership  
- Identification of failures  
- Severity assessment  
- Escalation thresholds  
- Reporting lines  
- Remediation tracking  
- Documentation requirements  

It applies to **all finance processes**, including close, reporting, tax, payroll, cash, and audit.

## Core Activities in This SOP

### Control Environment
- maintain preventive, detective, and corrective controls  
- ensure consistent application  

### Issue Identification
- detect breaches or weaknesses  
- assess financial, operational, or compliance impact  

### Escalation Triggers
Examples include:
- material misstatements  
- regulatory non-compliance  
- suspected fraud  
- repeated failures  

### Escalation Process
- document root cause  
- notify Finance Manager  
- escalate to senior leadership where required  
- define remediation plan and deadlines  

### Monitoring & Closure
- track progress  
- confirm resolution  
- prevent recurrence
