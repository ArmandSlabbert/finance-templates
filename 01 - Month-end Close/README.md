# SOP 01 – Month-End Close

## Overview
This folder contains the Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) and
supporting tools governing the month-end close process.

The objective is to ensure timely, accurate, and complete financial
reporting through a structured close timetable, clearly defined
responsibilities, and robust review controls.

## Contents

### SOP
• **SOP_01_Month_End_Close.docx**  
Defines the end-to-end month-end close process, including planning,
execution, review, and final sign-off.

### Operational Checklist
• **Month_End_Close_Checklist.xlsx**  
A practical, step-by-step checklist used to track month-end close
activities, assign ownership, monitor status, and evidence completion.

## Key Areas Covered
• Close planning and timetable management  
• Data completeness and cut-off controls  
• Journal processing and approvals  
• Balance sheet reconciliations  
• Variance analysis and review  
• Management sign-off  

## How This Is Used
The SOP provides the control framework, while the checklist is used
operationally during each reporting cycle to ensure consistency,
accountability, and audit-ready documentation.

## Outputs
• Closed general ledger for the reporting period  
• Management accounts  
• Supporting schedules and reconciliations  

## Standards
IFRS – Accrual Basis of Accounting  
IAS 1 – Presentation of Financial Statements
