# SOP 03 – Balance Sheet Reconciliations

## Overview
This Standard Operating Procedure defines the controls and
processes for preparing, reviewing, and maintaining balance
sheet reconciliations.

The objective is to ensure all balance sheet accounts are
accurate, complete, and supported by appropriate evidence.

## Key Areas
• Reconciliation frequency and ownership  
• Supporting documentation requirements  
• Review and sign-off controls  
• Identification and resolution of reconciling items  
• Ageing and follow-up of outstanding items  

## Accounts Covered
• Bank and cash  
• VAT and tax control accounts  
• Payroll and statutory balances  
• Accruals and pre-payments  
• Other balance sheet accounts  

## Standards
IFRS – Accrual Basis of Accounting  
IAS 1 – Presentation of Financial Statements
